Frequently Asked Questions


GoRemind is an app designed to remind users at the proper time and location. Utilizing users' current location and sound feedback, we aim to provide a useful reminder app that doesn't get ignored easily.

The development team consists of UC San Diego students Liuyi Chen, Francisco Ochoa, Kai-Chin Shih, and Yujie Wang.

How can I add a new task?

To add a new task, click on the "ADD" button on the top right.

How can I delete a task?

To delete a task, click on the garbage can icon.

What notification options do I have?

Follow phone notification settings.

Play through headphones.

Reminded only by vibration.

How can I log out?

To log out, click on the "LOG OUT" button on the top left.
